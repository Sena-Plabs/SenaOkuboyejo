About Me

Dr. Sena Okuboyejo

Assistant Professor of MIS, product-minded researcher, and EdTech innovator blending analytics, health informatics, and human-centered design.

Background

With over 17 years of experience spanning teaching, research, and program design, I focus on practical, evidence-based approaches to improve learning and decision-making. My work bridges the gap between academic rigor and real-world application, particularly in the domains of data analytics education, AI integration, and health informatics.

Currently serving as Assistant Professor of MIS at Metropolitan State University, I lead initiatives in analytics education, supervise doctoral students, and pioneer institutional adoption of generative AI technologies.

Research Focus

Mental Health Technology

Analyzing mobile health applications using machine learning and qualitative methods to improve digital mental health interventions.

Educational Innovation

Designing inclusive curricula and learning experiences that make data analytics accessible to diverse student populations.

AI Ethics & Adoption

Developing frameworks for responsible AI integration in higher education and training faculty on ethical AI use.
